French Onion Soup

We have been trying French onion soup in restaurants for years and my family and friends agree none can compare to my recipe for taste and simplicity of preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cups sliced onions
  • 4 (10.5 ounce) cans beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons dry sherry (optional)
  • 2teaspoon dried thyme salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices French bread 4 slices
  • 4 slices French bread
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Direction

  1. Melt butter with olive oil
  2. 8 quart stock pot on medium heat.
  3. Add onions and continually stir until tender and translucent.
  4. Do not brown the onions
  5. Add beef broth, sherry and thyme.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 30 minutes
  7. Ladle soup into oven safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each.
  8. Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolone, 1/2 slice diced Swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese.
